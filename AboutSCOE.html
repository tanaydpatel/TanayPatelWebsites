About Institute

Prof. M. N. Navale has established Sinhgad Technical Education Society (STES) in the year 1993 with the aim of imparting quality education in the field of Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, Medical, Physiotherapy, Nursing, Architecture, Hotel Management and School Education. At present, Sinhgad Technical Education Society has 13 campuses having 110 institutes and celebrating 25 years of academic excellence.

STES’s Sinhgad College of Engineering,Pune (SCOE) established in 1996, has become synonymous with quality engineering education. Presently SCOE offers courses in E&TC, Computer, IT, Mechanical, Chemical, Civil, Biotechnology, Production engineering and MBA. The courses are approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. Institute is Accredited ‘A’ Grade by NAAC. The institute conducts 14 post graduate programs and Ph. D. research center for 4 engineering disciplines.



About Departments

Department of Electronics and Telecommunication (UG Intake-240 PG Intake) : is accredited two times by NBA. The department also has a research center affiliated to SPPU. Liaison with prominent industries like ACTEL INC. (USA), KPIT, Texas Instruments, ST Microelectronics, Infosys, Intel, Nicrome, etc.

Department Information Technology (UG Intake-120) : was accredited two times by NBA. It is one of the pioneers in starting the course in University of Pune. The I.T. curriculum mainly focuses on new paradigms of the IT industry’s i.e. Software management, Software Engineering, Software Quality Control, Software Testing, Internet of Things (IoT), Machine Learning (ML), Artificial Intelligence (AI) and high-end technologies.

Department of Computer Engineering (UG Intake-120) : was accredited by NBA. The goal of the department is to establish state-of-the-art computing environment to develop competent computer engineers imbibed with the spirit of professionalism and responsible citizenship.
